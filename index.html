1. Executive Summary

CircuitAura is a premier PCB assembly company dedicated to providing high-quality, efficient, and cost-effective PCB solutions. Our mission is to deliver top-tier services in PCB assembly, from design and prototyping to mass production, with an emphasis on quality and customer satisfaction.

We aim to become a leading name in the PCB assembly industry by consistently exceeding client expectations with innovative and reliable solutions. Our commitment to excellence and customer-focused approach sets us apart in the competitive electronics manufacturing landscape.

2. Company Description

CircuitAura offers a comprehensive range of PCB services, including the assembly of single-layer, multi-layer, and flexible PCBs. We provide end-to-end solutions, including design assistance, prototyping, assembly, testing, and final inspection.

Our target market includes small to medium-sized enterprises in the electronics industry, as well as innovative startups looking to prototype their designs. With our state-of-the-art facility in Pune, India, we're equipped to handle projects of any complexity with precision and efficiency.

4. Market Research & Analysis

The PCB market is experiencing rapid growth with increasing demand across electronics, automotive, and telecommunications sectors. The global PCB market is projected to reach $89.7 billion by 2027, growing at a CAGR of 5.3%.

CircuitAura focuses on quality, affordability, and reliable turnaround to maintain a competitive edge. Key market opportunities include:

  • Growing IoT device market requiring specialized PCBs with advanced capabilities
  • Increased demand for flexible and rigid-flex PCBs in wearable technology
  • Rise of electric vehicles requiring advanced PCBs for battery management systems
  • Expansion of 5G infrastructure needing high-frequency PCBs for next-gen networks
